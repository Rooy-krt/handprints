Our Programs & Ministries

Every program is an expression of Christ’s love — reaching hearts, restoring hope, and transforming lives.

1) Bushfire Ministry

Bushfire Ministry serves as the umbrella body for Handprints Society’s outreach in Pokot. Founded with a vision to transform remote and marginalized communities, it embodies love, hope, compassion, and transformation tailored to the needs of West Pokot. Through this arm, we coordinate projects addressing education, spiritual growth, healthcare, and community development — facilitating sustainable change and expanding our reach.

Under Bushfire Ministry, we operate two primary initiatives:

i) Bushfire New Vision School – Pokot

In Karia village, West Pokot, where children once had no access to education, God enabled us to establish Bushfire New Vision Academy. What began with a few children learning under a tree has grown into a school serving 65 children — providing quality education, a feeding program, supplies, and spiritual nurture. Daily porridge and meals encourage attendance and protect children from early marriage, illiteracy, and hopelessness. We are committed to building classrooms, hiring teachers, and empowering children to break the cycle of poverty.

ii) West Pokot Outreach Center

In Karia village, West Pokot, we responded to God’s call to love those deemed unreachable. In a land where early marriages and lack of healthcare are prevalent, we planted seeds of transformation. We dream of building a full school and dispensary to serve this community holistically.

2) Widows Christmas Food Basket

What began as a small outreach to ensure widows and orphans do not go hungry during Christmas has grown, by God’s grace, from serving 20 widows to over 1,000 every year. Each widow receives a basket filled with rice, flour, beans, maize, sugar, tea leaves, soap, salt, matchsticks, and cooking oil — a celebration of Christ’s love for the women closest to His heart.

3) Free Medical Camps & Jigger Campaigns

Through our medical missions, we bring Christ’s healing touch to communities with little or no healthcare access. We offer free check-ups, treatments, basic cancer screenings, eye care, and health education. In Western Kenya, our jigger campaigns have restored dignity and greatly reduced infestations, giving communities renewed hope.

4) Education Project

Education unlocks destiny. We support children from disadvantaged backgrounds with school fees, uniforms, supplies, and mentorship — from primary to university level. Our mission is to bridge the gap between a child’s current challenges and the bright future God has prepared for them.

5) House Building Ministry

Many widows and vulnerable families live in unsafe shelters. God placed a burden on our hearts to restore dignity by building secure, permanent homes. Every completed home stands as a testimony of His love and faithfulness.

6) Famine Relief Program

During seasons of drought or financial hardship, we provide food supplies to families facing hunger. This ministry offers nourishment and encouragement, reminding families that God sees and cares for them.

7) King’s Kids Program

This program nurtures the spiritual growth of children through Bible studies, fun activities, mentorship, and discipleship. We teach children that they are loved by God, created with purpose, and capable of becoming strong, godly leaders.

8) Dorcas Empowerment Project

Inspired by the biblical Dorcas, this project empowers widows through skills training and business support. We provide seed capital, mentorship, and training in activities like tailoring, beadwork, farming, and retail — helping widows achieve economic independence and restored hope.

About Handprints Society

We exist to touch lives with the love of Christ — serving the vulnerable, empowering communities, and spreading hope wherever God sends us.
